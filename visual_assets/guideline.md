# Co-pilot Guideline (for Marp .md files)

## General
- Always follow NorthCode’s brand guidelines.
- Keep slides minimal, clear, and professional. Avoid clutter.

## Assets
- **Fonts** are located in the `fonts/` folder.  
  - IBM Plex Serif Regular (`fonts/IBMPlexSerif-Regular.ttf`)  
  - IBM Plex Mono Regular (`fonts/IBMPlexMono-Regular.ttf`)  
- **Images** are located in the `images/` folder.  
- **Logos** are located in the `images/logos/` folder.  
- These folders are on the same level as this `.md` file.  
- Always reference fonts and images relative to this structure.

## Logos
- Use only official logos (horizontal, stacked, compact) without modification.  
- Respect safe areas: equal to the height of “N” (or ½ height in compact logo).  
- **Placement**:  
  - Top-left for regular slides.  
  - Bottom-left for poster-style slides.  
  - Centered for opening/closing slides.  
- **Allowed combinations**:  
  - Black logo on mint background (`#a9f5e4`).  
  - Mint logo on black (`#1c1c1c`).  
  - White logo on black.  
  - Black logo on white.  
  - On calm images only if contrast is strong.  
- Never place logo on distracting backgrounds.

## Colors
- **Primary palette**:  
  - Mint `#a9f5e4` (accent color).  
  - Black `#1c1c1c`.  
  - White `#f5f5f5`.  
- Use mint sparingly as an accent.  
- Prefer monochrome (black/white) slides with mint highlights.

## Typography
- Use **IBM Plex Serif Regular** for headlines.  
- Use **IBM Plex Mono Regular** for body text, captions, tables, and charts.  
- Fonts must be loaded from the `fonts/` folder.  
- Use regular weights only. Bold only if absolutely necessary.  
- Headlines must be 2–3× larger than body text.  
- Limit to max. 2 text sizes per slide.  
- Tracking: Serif -25/1000 em, Mono 0.  
- Keep typography clear, strong, and high contrast.

## Layout
- Use simple grid-based layouts (4-column system if multi-column).  
- Keep margins generous and consistent.  
- **Alignment**:  
  - Top-left logo for content slides.  
  - Centered logo for title/closing slides.  
- **Charts & tables**:  
  - Strip away unnecessary lines, boxes, or colors.  
  - Black/white by default.  
  - Mint only as highlight.

## Slide types
- **Opening slide**: Centered logo (from `images/logos/`), mint background, large headline.  
- **Content slide**: Top-left or bottom-left logo, white or black background, clear headline + body.  
- **Closing slide**: Centered logo (from `images/logos/`), “Thank you” message.
